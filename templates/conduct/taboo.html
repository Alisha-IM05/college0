How Review Filtering Works

  • 0 taboo words — review published normally ✅
  • 1–2 taboo words — words replaced with asterisks (***), student gets 1 warning ⚠️
  • 3+ taboo words — review is hidden entirely, student gets 2 warnings 🚫

Non-registrar users only ever see the asterisk version — never the original words. As registrar, you can see both the filtered and original text.
